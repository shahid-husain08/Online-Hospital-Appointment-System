About LifeLine Hospital

Committed to excellence in healthcare with over 25 years of trusted service to our community. Our journey is one of compassion, innovation, and healing.

Our Story

Providing Exceptional Care Since 1998

Founded by Dr. Robert Chen and a team of dedicated medical professionals, LifeLine Hospital began as a modest 50-bed facility with a vision to transform healthcare in our region.

Today, we stand as a 300-bed multi-specialty hospital, recognized for our state-of-the-art facilities and personalized approach to medicine.

Our journey has been guided by our core values of compassion, integrity, and excellence. Every year, we treat over 100,000 patients with the same level of care we would want for our own families.

In 2022, we were awarded the prestigious "Best Patient Care Hospital" in the state, a testament to our continuous efforts to raise the standard of healthcare.

Our Guiding Principles

Our Mission

To provide exceptional, comprehensive healthcare services with compassion and respect for human dignity. We are committed to advancing medical excellence through innovation, while maintaining affordability and accessibility for all members of our community.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the regional leader in patient-centered healthcare, setting new standards in clinical excellence and pioneering medical research. We aspire to create a healthier community by integrating cutting-edge technology with personalized, compassionate care.

Our Values

Compassion: Treating every patient with empathy and kindness. Excellence: Pursuing the highest standards in all we do. Integrity: Maintaining honesty and ethical practice. Innovation: Embracing advancement in medical science. Community: Serving with a spirit of togetherness.

Our World-Class Facilities

Advanced Surgical Suites

Our 12 operating rooms feature the latest in minimally invasive technology, including robotic-assisted surgery systems and advanced imaging integration.

Level IV NICU

Our neonatal intensive care unit is staffed 24/7 by board-certified neonatologists and equipped to care for the most fragile newborns with the highest survival rates in the region.

Cardiac Care Center

Featuring a dedicated cardiac catheterization lab, electrophysiology lab, and comprehensive rehabilitation program for complete heart care.

Comprehensive Cancer Center

Our cancer program includes radiation oncology, medical oncology, and surgical oncology, supported by a multidisciplinary tumor board and patient navigators.

Rehabilitation Institute

Our 15,000 sq ft rehabilitation center offers physical, occupational, and speech therapy with aquatic therapy pools and specialized equipment for neurological recovery.

24/7 Emergency Care

Our emergency department is staffed by board-certified emergency physicians and features dedicated trauma bays, pediatric emergency area, and rapid evaluation zones.
